The Context Game is released under the MIT license. You can fork it, modify it, run your own instance. But forking means starting from scratch — your players won't have access to this game's questions, entries, or community.

This game was designed so you don't need a fork. Everything is open, everything is permanent, and anyone can participate in this instance. Forking is a safety valve: if I got something fundamentally wrong, or you have a completely different vision for how a knowledge competition should work, you have that option.

But if you just want to play — there's no need to fork. The game is already open. Show up, compete, and help build the record of what people think.
